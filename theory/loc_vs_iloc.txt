.iloc[]
=======

Integer-position-based indexing
Accesses rows and columns by integer position, just like in a NumPy array.
Uses zero-based integer indexes.

ðŸ§¾ Sample DataFrame:

	name	age
a	Alice	25

c	Charlie	35
